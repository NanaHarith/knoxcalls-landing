SMS Compliance & Consent

How KnoxCalls Night Watch obtains and manages end-user consent for SMS communications.

Overview

KnoxCalls Night Watch sends SMS messages for two purposes: service updates to business owners who sign up on our website, and emergency dispatch alerts to on-call technicians enrolled through the Night Watch Dashboard. Both consent flows require explicit end-user action before any messages are sent.

Consent Principle

No SMS message is ever sent to a phone number that has not been explicitly confirmed by the end-user who owns that number. Business owners cannot unilaterally enroll a technician—the technician must independently confirm via SMS reply.

Consent Methods

Method 1

Website SMS Opt-In

Business owners and prospects visit knoxcalls.com and submit the SMS signup form. The form requires:

  • Name, email, and phone number
  • Checking the explicit consent checkbox: "I want to receive SMS text messages from KnoxCalls Night Watch"
  • Clicking "Sign Up for SMS Updates"

Messages include pilot program availability, service announcements, and account notifications.

Method 2

Technician Dispatch Enrollment

Business owners add technicians to their dispatch roster via the Night Watch Dashboard. The owner confirms:

  • The technician has been informed about the SMS alerts
  • A consent checkbox: "I confirm this technician has consented to receive automated dispatch alerts. An opt-in confirmation text will be sent."

The technician must then independently confirm via the SMS handshake described below.

Technician Dispatch Opt-In Flow

When a business owner adds a technician to their emergency dispatch roster, the following sequence occurs:

1
Owner Initiates The business owner opens the Night Watch Dashboard, navigates to "Manage Technicians," and clicks "Add Technician." They enter the technician's name and phone number, then check the consent confirmation box.
2
Opt-In SMS Sent The system sends an opt-in confirmation SMS to the technician's phone number. The technician is not yet enrolled—no dispatch alerts are sent until confirmation.
3
Technician Confirms The technician replies START to confirm they wish to receive emergency dispatch alerts. This reply is logged with a timestamp for compliance records.
4
Alerts Activated Only after the technician replies START are they added to the active dispatch roster. They will now receive emergency dispatch alerts when the AI system triages an urgent call for their assigned business.

Opt-In Confirmation SMS

The following is the exact text of the opt-in confirmation SMS sent to technicians:

KnoxCalls: You've been added to [Company Name]'s emergency dispatch roster. Reply START to confirm you wish to receive urgent alerts on this number. Msg&data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help.

If the technician does not reply START, they remain in a "pending" state and will not receive any dispatch messages.

SMS Keywords & Opt-Out

All KnoxCalls SMS messages support the following standard keywords:

Keyword Action
START Confirms opt-in and activates SMS alerts for the number.
STOP Immediately opts the user out of all SMS messages. A final confirmation SMS is sent. No further messages will be delivered.
HELP Returns support information and a link to contact KnoxCalls at info@knoxcalls.com.

Message Details

  • Program Name: KnoxCalls Night Watch
  • Message Types: Emergency dispatch alerts, service updates, account notifications, and opt-in/opt-out confirmations.
  • Message Frequency: Varies based on call volume and account activity. Dispatch alerts are sent only when the AI triages an urgent after-hours call.
  • Cost: Message and data rates may apply. KnoxCalls does not charge for SMS messages, but standard carrier rates apply.
  • Third-Party Sharing: Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Consent Is Not a Condition of Purchase

Your consent to receive text messages from KnoxCalls Night Watch is not required as a condition of purchasing any goods or services. You may use Night Watch without opting in to SMS alerts, though doing so may limit dispatch notification functionality.

How to Rejoin

If you previously opted out by replying STOP and wish to receive messages again:

  • Reply START or UNSTOP from the same mobile number to re-enroll in SMS alerts.
  • Alternatively, contact us at info@knoxcalls.com and request to be re-enrolled.
  • You may also opt in again via our SMS Opt-In page.
