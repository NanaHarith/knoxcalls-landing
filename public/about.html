The Problem with "Anytime"

Every contractor knows the sound. The phone rings at 2:00 AM. It’s a tenant with a "problem."

Is it a burst pipe flooding the basement? Or is it a dripping faucet they just noticed?

For years, answering services have promised to help. But they're usually in a call center in another state (or country), reading from a script, terrified to make a decision. So they wake you up. For everything.
