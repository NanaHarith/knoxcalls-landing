SMS Compliance & Consent

How KnoxCalls Night Watch obtains and manages end-user consent for SMS communications.

Overview

KnoxCalls Night Watch sends SMS messages to the business owner who signed up for the service. In the base Night Watch plan, the owner is also the on-call contact — they receive emergency dispatch alerts and service notifications on the same number they registered with.

Consent Principle

No SMS message is ever sent to a phone number that has not explicitly opted in. The business owner provides consent at the time of signup via the website form. All dispatch alerts and service notifications go to that consented number only.

How Consent Is Collected

Website Opt-In

Pilot Program Request Form

The business owner visits knoxcalls.com and submits the pilot access form. SMS consent requires:

  • Entering name, phone number, and business details
  • Checking the explicit SMS consent checkbox: "I agree to receive SMS text messages from KnoxCalls Night Watch regarding pilot program availability, service announcements, and account notifications."
  • Clicking "Request Pilot Access"

The checkbox is unchecked by default. Consent is not a condition of purchase. Full disclosure language — including message frequency, data rates, STOP/HELP instructions, and links to Privacy Policy and Terms of Service — is displayed inline with the checkbox.

Opt-In Flow

1
Owner Submits Form The business owner visits knoxcalls.com/sms-opt-in or the pilot request form at knoxcalls.com, enters their name and mobile number, checks the SMS consent checkbox, and submits.
2
Consent Recorded The opt-in is logged with a timestamp. No SMS is sent to the number until the owner's Night Watch account is active.
3
Messages Begin Once active, the owner receives emergency dispatch alerts and service notifications on their registered number. They can reply STOP at any time to opt out.

Welcome SMS

The following confirmation is sent when the owner's account goes live:

KnoxCalls Night Watch: You're all set. Night Watch is active and monitoring your calls. Msg freq varies. Msg&data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help.

SMS Keywords & Opt-Out

All KnoxCalls SMS messages support the following standard keywords:

Keyword Action
START Confirms opt-in and activates SMS alerts for the number.
STOP Immediately opts the user out of all SMS messages. A final confirmation SMS is sent. No further messages will be delivered.
HELP Returns support information and a link to contact KnoxCalls at info@knoxcalls.com.

Message Details

  • Program Name: KnoxCalls Night Watch
  • Message Types: Emergency dispatch alerts, service updates, account notifications, and opt-in/opt-out confirmations.
  • Message Frequency: Varies based on call volume and account activity. Dispatch alerts are sent only when the AI triages an urgent after-hours call.
  • Cost: Message and data rates may apply. KnoxCalls does not charge for SMS messages, but standard carrier rates apply.
  • Third-Party Sharing: Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Consent Is Not a Condition of Purchase

Your consent to receive text messages from KnoxCalls Night Watch is not required as a condition of purchasing any goods or services. You may use Night Watch without opting in to SMS alerts, though doing so may limit dispatch notification functionality.

How to Rejoin

If you previously opted out by replying STOP and wish to receive messages again:

  • Reply START or UNSTOP from the same mobile number to re-enroll in SMS alerts.
  • Alternatively, contact us at info@knoxcalls.com and request to be re-enrolled.
  • You may also opt in again via our SMS Opt-In page.
