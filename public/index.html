Questions Contractors Ask

How does Night Watch know what's an emergency?

It asks the right questions—what's happening, how urgent, what equipment is involved. A flooded basement at a hotel gets escalated. A "just checking prices" call gets logged for morning. You can customize the rules.

Will callers know they're not talking to a person?

Most can't tell. Night Watch answers naturally, uses your business name, and handles conversations like your best dispatcher would. We've tested it—people think they're talking to your team.

What if I don't respond to an emergency alert?

With the Bulldog add-on, Night Watch keeps trying. Text, then call, then your backup contact. It doesn't stop until someone acknowledges. Without Bulldog, you get text alerts and can respond on your schedule.

Can I change when Night Watch is active?

Absolutely. Forward when you're done for the day, un-forward when you start. Most contractors do 5 PM to 7 AM plus weekends. But you're in control.

Do I need to change my business phone number?

No. You keep your number. Just forward it to Night Watch when you want coverage. Your customers call the same number they always have.

What's the catch with the founding member rate?

No catch. We're building this with real contractors and want feedback. In exchange, you get half-price for life. When the pilot spots fill up, the rate goes to $299. If it's not for you, cancel anytime.
