Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

IMPORTANT: This Privacy Policy describes how KNOXCALLS, LLC ("Night Watch," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use our AI-powered telephone answering and emergency dispatch services.

1. Introduction

At KnoxCalls Night Watch, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in compliance with applicable laws and carrier requirements.

Night Watch provides AI-powered telephone answering, call screening, emergency triage, and dispatch notification services (the "Service"). By using our Service, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you provide directly to us when you apply for our pilot program, request a quote, or configure your account. This includes:

  • Business Entity Name and Contact Details
  • Authorized Representative Name, Email, and Phone Number
  • Service preferences, dispatch instructions, and on-call schedules
  • Payment information (processed securely by third-party processors)

2.2 Customer Proprietary Network Information (CPNI)

In providing our services, we may collect Customer Proprietary Network Information ("CPNI"). CPNI includes information related to the quantity, technical configuration, type, destination, location, and amount of use of a telecommunications service. We protect CPNI in accordance with federal laws and regulations. We do not sell, trade, or share CPNI with third parties for marketing purposes without your express consent, except as required by law or to provide the Service.

2.3 Call Recording Compliance

Notice of Quality Assurance and Legal Compliance:

  • Service Recording: To ensure service quality, accurate emergency dispatch, and training, all calls handled by Night Watch are recorded. This includes audio of conversations between callers and our AI system.
  • Tennessee Law: We record calls in compliance with Tennessee law, which allows for recording with one-party consent.
  • Notice Provided: Our system provides verbal notice at the beginning of each call that the interaction is being recorded.
  • Caller Consent: By continuing the call after receiving this notice, callers consent to the recording of the conversation.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our AI dispatch services.
  • Process transactions and send related information, including confirmations and invoices.
  • Send technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support and administrative messages.
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests.
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Service.
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities.

4. SMS Communication & Consent

4.1 Opt-In Information

KnoxCalls Night Watch provides emergency dispatch notifications and account alerts via SMS.

  • Consent Flow: End-users consent to receive SMS messages by providing their phone number through our web-based enrollment forms or account setup pages.
  • Explicit Agreement: By entering your phone number and clicking "Submit" or checking the designated consent box, you agree to receive automated service alerts from KnoxCalls.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

4.2 Message Details

  • Program Description: Alerts for emergency calls, service updates, and account notifications.
  • Message Frequency: Frequency varies based on call volume and account activity.
  • Cost: Message and data rates may apply.
  • Opt-Out: Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. We will send one final confirmation SMS.
  • Support: Reply HELP for assistance or email info@knoxcalls.com.

5. Sharing of Information

We strictly limit how we share your information. We may share your data in the following circumstances:

5.1 Third-Party Sub-Processors

We engage trusted third-party vendors to help us provide the Service. These vendors are authorized to use your personal information only as necessary to provide these services to us. Categories of sub-processors include:

  • Telephony & CPaaS Providers: (e.g., Telnyx, Twilio) To facilitate calls and SMS.
  • AI & Transcription Services: (e.g., Vapi, OpenAI) To process voice audio and generate transcripts.
  • Cloud Infrastructure: (e.g., Railway, AWS) To host our secure application data.

5.2 Legal Compliance

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to:

  • Comply with a legal obligation, subpoena, or court order.
  • Protect and defend the rights or property of KnoxCalls, LLC.
  • Prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the Service.
  • Protect the personal safety of users of the Service or the public.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption of data in transit (TLS) and at rest, role-based access controls, and regular security reviews.
