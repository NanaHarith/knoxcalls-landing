Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

IMPORTANT: This Privacy Policy describes how KNOXCALLS, LLC ("Night Watch," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use our AI-powered telephone answering and emergency dispatch services.

1. Introduction

At KnoxCalls Night Watch (\"we,\" \"us,\" or \"our\"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Night Watch provides AI-powered telephone answering, call screening, emergency triage, and dispatch notification services (the "Service") primarily for HVAC companies, property maintenance businesses, and apartment management companies.

By using our Service, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you provide directly to us when you apply for our pilot program, request a quote, or use our dispatch services. This includes:

  • Name and Business Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Service preferences and dispatch instructions
  • Emergency contact information
  • Payment information

2.2 Call Recording and Information Collected Automatically

CALL RECORDING NOTICE:

  • All calls handled by Night Watch are recorded. This includes the complete audio of conversations between callers and our AI system.
  • Tennessee Law: Tennessee is a "one-party consent" state. This means that as a party to the conversation, we have the legal right to record calls.
  • Notice Provided: Our AI system provides verbal notice at the beginning of each call that the call may be recorded.
  • Caller Consent: By continuing the call after receiving notice, callers consent to recording.

When you use our Service, we automatically collect:

  • Call Data: Caller phone numbers, call duration, call time and date, call recordings, call transcripts
  • Service Usage: Number of calls received, call classifications (emergency vs. non-emergency), response times, dispatch notifications sent
  • Device and Connection Information: IP address, browser type, operating system when visiting our website

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses minimal tracking technologies:

  • Essential Cookies: We may use strictly necessary cookies to enable basic website functionality (e.g., form submissions).
  • Analytics: We may use basic analytics to understand website traffic and improve our Service. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.
  • No Third-Party Advertising: We do not use advertising cookies or sell data to advertisers.

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain website functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our after-hours dispatch services
  • Answer and screen calls on your behalf
  • Classify calls as emergency or non-emergency
  • Dispatch emergency notifications to designated contacts
  • Provide call logs, transcripts, and analytics
  • Process payments and maintain billing records
  • Communicate with you regarding service alerts, emergency dispatches, and billing
  • Improve our AI's ability to understand emergencies and enhance conversation quality
  • Process your application for our pilot program

4. SMS Communication

Strict A2P 10DLC Compliance Statement:

We value your privacy. No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

5.1 Service Providers

We share information with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf, including:

  • Cloud hosting and secure storage
  • Telecommunications providers (e.g., Twilio, telephony providers)
  • AI Services for speech recognition and natural language processing
  • Payment processing services
  • Customer support systems

These service providers are contractually required to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

5.2 Emergency Dispatch

When we identify an emergency, we share relevant call information (caller details, nature of emergency) with your designated emergency contacts via SMS or phone call as configured in your account.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, subpoena, court order, or government request, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect and defend our rights or property
  • Prevent fraud or security issues
  • Protect the safety of our users or the public

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Access Controls: Role-based access restrictions limit who can access your data
  • Secure Infrastructure: Industry-standard cloud hosting with security certifications
  • Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for security threats

Note: While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Update your account information at any time through your account dashboard

7.2 Deletion

You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain limitations. We may retain information necessary to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our Service:

  • Call Recordings: Retained for 90 days by default
  • Call Transcripts and Logs: Retained for 90 days by default
  • Account Information: Retained for the duration of your account plus 1 year after account closure
  • Payment Information: Retained as required by tax and accounting laws (typically 7 years)

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is designed for business-to-business use and is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at info@knoxcalls.com and we will delete such information from our systems.
