Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 14, 2026

At KnoxCalls Night Watch ("we," "us," or "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you apply for our pilot program, request a quote, or use our dispatch services. This includes:

  • Name and Business Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Service preferences and dispatch instructions

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our after-hours dispatch services.
  • Communicate with you regarding service alerts, emergency dispatches, and billing.
  • Process your application for our pilot program.

3. No Sharing of Mobile Information

Strict A2P 10DLC Compliance Statement:

We value your privacy. No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

4. SMS Communication

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive SMS messages from us regarding your account and service activities (e.g., "New Emergency Call" alerts). You may opt-out of these communications at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance. Message frequency varies based on your service volume. Message and data rates may apply.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@knoxcalls.com.
